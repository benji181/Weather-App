Weather Application

Welcome to the Weather App! This application allows you to check the current weather conditions and get a 7-day forecast for any location around the world. You can also automatically fetch weather based on your current location and receive alerts for severe weather conditions like storms and extreme temperatures. Customize the theme to suit your preference by switching between light and dark mode. Stay updated and prepared, no matter where you are!
